Plan window

Always avoid

  • Cow's milk dairy — milk, butter, yogurt, cheese, cream, whey, casein.
  • Carrots — known reaction; skip in purées, sauces and finger foods.
  • Oats & bread — skip for now; main carbs are rice and white pasta.
  • Honey — never before 12 months.
  • Added salt & sugar — not suitable for babies.
  • Whole nuts, large chunks, whole grapes — choking risk.

Gentle gut rules

  • Tough first, easy last. Meal 1 is the heaviest, highest-fibre meal. Each later meal is lighter on the stomach.
  • 3 days in a row, never alternate. Every food — fruit, veggie, egg, PB, meat — runs 3 consecutive days before rotating.
  • Banana, sparingly. Banana can be heavy and constipating for sensitive guts — introduced only from CW28 (mid-July) and rotated with gentler fruits (avocado, peach, plum, pear, blueberry).
  • Allergen rhythm: eggs Mon–Wed · peanut butter Thu–Sat · Sunday rest. Tiles with egg or peanut butter are marked with a dark-green pill.
  • Caloric needs: 6 months ~600 kcal/day total · 7–8 months ~650–700 kcal/day · 9+ months ~750 kcal/day. Solids contribute ~200 kcal/day at 6m, ~300 kcal/day at 9m — the rest comes from breast milk / hypoallergenic formula. Each tile shows an estimated kcal value; each day card shows the daily solids total.
  • Cook everything well. Peel, deseed and mash or finely shred.
  • Iron-rich foods at Meal 1 every day (lentils, then meats from week 5).
  • Continue 500–700 ml/day breast milk or hypoallergenic formula; sips of water at every meal.
