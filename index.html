Plan window

Always avoid

  • Cow's milk dairy — no cow's milk, butter, yogurt, cheese, cream, whey, casein. Read labels.
  • Carrots — reported reaction; skip in purees and finger foods.
  • Honey — never before 12 months (botulism risk).
  • Added salt & sugar — not suitable for babies.
  • Whole nuts, large chunks, whole grapes — choking risk.

Easy on the tummy

  • Introduce one new food at a time and wait 3 days before the next new one.
  • Start with small amounts (1–2 tsp). Increase only if stools and skin stay calm.
  • Cook well, peel, deseed, blend smooth at first. Thicken textures gradually.
  • Keep iron-rich foods daily (meat, lentils, fortified oats).
  • Continue breast milk or hypoallergenic formula on demand — food is for learning, milk still feeds.
